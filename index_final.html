Baseball Players: left-handed vs right-handed

Batting Average

In baseball, the batting average is defined by the number of hits divided by at bats. Source

The mean batting average of right-handed players is lower than left-handed and ambidextrous players.

Home Runs

In baseball, a home run is scored when the ball is hit in such a way that the batter is able to circle
the bases and reach home safely in one play without any errors being committed by
the defensive team in the process. Source

Left-handed players have a higher number of home runs than the other players.
